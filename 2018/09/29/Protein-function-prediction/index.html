Underlying challenges

  1. The complex relation between protein sequence, structure and function
  2. The large and complex output space for the classification method.
  3. Proteins do not function isolation.

GOLabler

According to the initial evaluation of CAFA3 in July 2017, GOLabeler achieved the first place in terms of F-max out of around 200 submissions by around 50 labs all over the world.

challenge

  1. structured ontology: GO terms are node labels of a directed acyclic graph (DAG), by which one gene annotated at one node must be labeled by GO terms of all ancestor nodes in the DAG.
  2. many labels per protein: 71~
  3. large variation in the number of GO terms per protein: also we found that only 634, out of all 10 236 GO terms in MFO (GO Ontology in June 2016), are associated with >50 proteins.
  4. imbalance

MS-KNN

challenges on multi-source function prediction.

  1. The first is that different sources of information may have vastly different coverage. For example, while sequence similarity covers all known proteins, PPI data coverage is significantly smaller, and gene expression similarities are constrained by a specific microarray platform.
  2. The second challenge is differences in data quality. For example, PPI can be obtained by a variety of techniques that differ in cost and reliability. A confounding issue is that functional annotations have an uneven coverage biased towards certain types of proteins and functions, and that determination of protein functions, such as the one provided by Gene Ontology [14], is a subjective and error-prone process.
