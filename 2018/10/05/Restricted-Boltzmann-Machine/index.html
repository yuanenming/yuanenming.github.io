There are some notes about the famous machine learning model: Restricted Boltzmann Machine(RBM), Which was one of the most basic neural networks.

Definition

RBMs are shallow, two-layer neural nets that constitute the building blocks of deep-belief networks. It is applied in dimensionality reduction, classification, regression, collaborative filtering, feature learning and topic modeling.
(As a generative model, RBMs are seldomly used compared with GAN or variational autoencoders.)

Structure


It has a simplest two layer structure. But not like the forward nets, It has a backward process, which was called as reconstruction.

Reconstructions


The reconstruction process can be illustrated by the above diagram.
To measure the distance between its estimated probability distribution and the ground-truth distribution of the input, RBMs use Kullback Leibler Divergence. A thorough explanation of the math can be found on Wikipedia.
